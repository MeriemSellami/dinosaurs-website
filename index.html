Tyrannosauridae
Tyrannosaurus rex
North America
Tyrannosaurus was a carnivore. It lived in the Cretaceous period and inhabited North America. Its fossils have been found in places such as Utah, Coahuila (Mexico), and Montana.

Ceratopsidae
Triceratops
North America
Triceratops was a herbivore. It lived in the Cretaceous period and inhabited North America. Its fossils have been found in places such as North Dakota, Wyoming, and Saskatchewan (Canada).
